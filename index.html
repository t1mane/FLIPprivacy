FLIP Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2025

Simple Summary: FLIP helps you discover activities in your city and connect with friends. We only collect the information necessary to make the app work - your location to show you nearby activities and your posts to share with others.

Information We Collect

Location Information

We collect your precise location when you use FLIP to:

  • Show you activities and posts near your current location
  • Add location tags to your posts so others can find them
  • Create zone-based chat rooms when areas become active

Content You Share

When you post on FLIP, we collect:

  • Photos and videos you choose to share
  • Text captions and messages
  • The location where you posted

Account Information

To create your account, we collect:

  • Username and emoji you choose
  • Email address (if provided)
  • Friends list (people you connect with on the app)

How We Use Your Information

We use your information only to provide FLIP's core features:

  • Show you relevant content: Display posts and activities near your location
  • Enable social features: Let you follow friends and see their posts
  • Create communities: Form zone-based chat rooms in active areas
  • Improve the app: Understand how people use FLIP to make it better

Information Sharing

Your information is shared in these ways:

  • Posts you make public: Visible to all FLIP users in your area
  • Posts you make private: Only visible to your friends
  • Zone chats: Messages are visible to others in the same area
  • We do not sell your data to advertisers or third parties

Data Retention

Posts on FLIP automatically delete after 24 hours. We may retain some data longer for:

  • Account information (until you delete your account)
  • Safety and security purposes
  • Legal compliance requirements

Your Choices

You control your privacy on FLIP:

  • Location sharing: Turn off in your device settings (app won't work without location)
  • Post visibility: Choose public or friends-only for each post
  • Friend connections: Accept or decline friend requests
  • Account deletion: Contact us to delete your account and data

Data Security

We protect your information using industry-standard security measures including encryption and secure servers. However, no internet service is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

FLIP is intended for users 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. We'll notify users of significant changes through the app or email.
